About Us

SATELIT is a community rooted in the love of books and discussion. Founded on June 9, 2021 by a group of students, SATELIT started as a small gathering every Saturday night. Today, the community has grown rapidly with over 300 students actively participating, as well as reaching a wider audience through Instagram with over 16.1k followers and over 20 million views of reels. SATELIT serves as a space for Indonesian students in Turkey, especially in Sakarya, to expand their horizons, improve their literacy, and build their English speaking skills through data-based discussions and education.

Our Mission

Growing literacy awareness among Indonesian students in Turkey, especially in Sakarya, with an active discussion approach, communication skills development through English Speaking Club, and engaging literacy education through social media.

Our Vision

Promoting literacy and education as the best long-term investment, forming a generation that is smart, critical thinking, and ready to contribute to the Golden Indonesia 2045.

Our Values

  • Literacy as a Foundation - We believe that reading and understanding are the first steps to progress.
  • Enlightening Discussions - Exchanging ideas and thoughts to build a critical and analytical mindset.
  • Global Communication - Through our English Speaking Club, we equip students with English language skills to compete internationally.
  • Digital Education - Utilizing social media as the main tool to spread knowledge and increase literacy awareness.
  • Commitment to Golden Indonesia 2045 (Indonesia Emas 2045) - Every step we take is oriented towards the advancement of Indonesia's young generation.

Our History

SATELIT was born from small discussions held every Saturday night, which later grew into a community with hundreds of active participants. Over time, we expanded our reach through social media, presenting engaging and interactive educational content. With a focus on literacy, discussion, English Speaking Club, and digital education, SATELIT continues to innovate to create an inclusive and dynamic learning ecosystem. We believe that big changes start with small steps, and SATELIT is part of the journey towards a smarter and more empowered Indonesia.
